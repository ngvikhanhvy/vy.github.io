Here are some of the best travel destinations in Vietnam!

Ha Long Bay

If you ever want to be amazed at the wonder of nature, Ha Long Bay is the place to be. Ha Long Bay is located in the northeast of Vietnam, which is about 165km from the capital of Ha Noi. Travelers come to Halong Bay for an up-close look at its amazing caves, rock formations and limestone islands.


It doesn't matter what your travelling type preference is, there is always something for you to do at Ha Long Bay. These are examples of what you can do here:


Take a cruise

Visit the caves

Sapa

Sapa is a remote town in northwestern Vietnam that's known for its pristine rice terraces, cool mountain atmosphere, and community of ethnic people full of cultural diversity. Sapa town stands at the head of a deep valley of magnificent rice terraces surrounded by a variety of wild animals like birds and squirrels. This is what you will probably hear when you get to Sapa!

Here are some top things you can do in Sapa:


Summit Mount Fansipan

Visit the Sapa ethnic market

Hoi An

Hoi An Ancient town is located in Viet Nams central Quang Nam Province. A once bustling trading port, Hoi An is nowadays a rustic and charming cultural magnet to any visitors.

Here are some top things you can do in Hoi An:


Get lost in the old town during Lantern Festival

Treat your tastebuds

Ly Son Island

Ly Son Island is a district within Quang Ngai, a central province which generally escapes the notice of most travellers. With warm weather all year round, Ly Son is quite tourism friendly. The sun is mild and warm from December to February - make your travel plans!

Here are some top things you can do in Ly Son:


Visit the To Vo stone gate

Learn about the traditional argricultural product of Ly Son - black garlic
